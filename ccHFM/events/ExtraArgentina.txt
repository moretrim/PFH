# The stage is as follows:
# - Rosas' rule is represented by an early-game dictatorship Confederation, or if his party
#   (reactionaries) is in power
# - Blanco-led Uruguay is represented by conservatives or reactionaries being in power in Uruguay,
#   and is treated as the sole regional political ally to Rosas
# - Colorado-led Uruguay is represented by liberals in power, and is treated as a political enemy of
#   Rosas
# - SBA is a revolt tag, normally representing the historical secession of Buenos Aires
# - Rosas' base of power is Buenos Aires, so SBA should never come up under Rosas
